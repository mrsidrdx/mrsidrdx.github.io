Event #1
PAANCHAJANYA 2K18

IEEE-GIT conducted its annual flagship event PAANCHAJANYA. This is a regional level inter-college technical symposium for students. Idea presentation, Projects, Hackathon, are major events. Student and Members were benefitted.

  • Resource Person : Dr. Anand S. Deshpande, Principal, KLS’s GIT. Dr. Santosh Saraf, Dept Chair, IEEEGIT Student Branch
  • Duration : 2 days
  • No. of Attendees
    • IEEE : 50
    • Non-IEEE : 350
Organised from 24-02-2018 to 25-02-2018
Event #2
TRIGARTI – Women’s Day Celebration

It was a part of IEEE-GIT WIE affinity group. Girl students get an opportunity to showcase their creativity and business skills. Chief Guest talked about challenges for women in today’s workplace. Ladies staffs of the institute were felicitated. It helped in membership of girl students.

  • Resource Person : Mrs. Sapna, Finance Officer, VTU Belagavi
  • Duration : 1 day (9am – 5pm)
  • No. of Attendees
    • IEEE : 30
    • Non-IEEE : 300
Organised on 08-03-2018

Event #5
STUDENT LEADERSHIP PROGRAM (SLP-2018)

This is an annual membership drive event. Target audience: 1st and 3rd Semester students are attracted for membership, by supporting best performing student with finance for membership. Faculty Advisor Prof. Abhishek Deshmukh presented about IEEE memberships its awareness, usage, advantages, opportunities to new students who took up membership. Student Branch decided to support 3 new members with nearly 50% IEEE membership fees support.

  • Resource Person : Prof. Abhishek Deshmukh
  • Duration : 1 day (9am – 5pm)
  • No. of Attendees
    • IEEE : 30
    • Non-IEEE : 125
Organised on 25-08-2018
Event #6
